# Sysilo

## What This Is

Sysilo is an enterprise integration intelligence platform that helps companies discover, organize, and operate all of their SaaS/data integrations in one place. It gives Ops/IT integration leads a unified view of integration health, ownership, dependencies, and data flow quality across systems. Sysilo differentiates with an AI integration copilot that helps teams understand issues faster and recommend high-confidence actions.

## Core Value

Enterprise teams can reliably see and govern their integration landscape, with AI assistance that reduces integration complexity and operational risk.

## Context

The product direction is enterprise-first and aimed at Ops/IT integration leads responsible for cross-system reliability and data consistency. Current intent is to position Sysilo as "close to Zapier" in integration utility, but differentiated by enterprise visibility, governance posture, and AI-guided integration operations rather than pure automation breadth. The immediate proof point for v1 is integration inventory plus health intelligence, with AI copilot capabilities layered into daily integration maintenance workflows.

## Constraints

- **Scope**: v1 must center on integration inventory + health visibility — this is the first measurable outcome
- **Positioning**: Compete adjacent to Zapier without attempting full feature parity in v1 — protects focus and delivery risk
- **User target**: Prioritize enterprise Ops/IT integration leads as primary users — design and requirements must optimize for their workflows
